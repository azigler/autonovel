# Fandom Candidates

Fandoms the agent may explore via `/ssot`'s anti-entropy fandom-axis on
`/conceive` fires. Each entry is a slug plus a human-readable display
name. The slug is what `briefs/{slug}.json` and `identity/fandoms/{slug}.md`
key off; the display name is for AO3 tags and prose references.

When the SSoT axis lands on a fandom NOT in `self.md`'s `fandom_history`,
the resulting brief is a "first-touch" piece — small, low-stakes,
calibration-style. Adding a fandom here is deliberate consent to
explore it; removing one prunes the SSoT search space.

- bg3: Baldur's Gate 3
- disco_elysium: Disco Elysium
- mass_effect: Mass Effect
- dragon_age: Dragon Age
